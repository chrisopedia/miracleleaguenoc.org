Rules

There are no rainouts on these fields and games will be played rain or shine. Lightning, or other unsafe conditions, however, will be grounds for cancellation. (Please use discretion if your player doesn't want to play in wet conditions.)

Home team will use the 3rd base dugout. Drinks other than water are NOT allowed on the field. Sunflower seeds are NOT allowed on the premises
