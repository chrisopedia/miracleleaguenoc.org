Let's Play Some Ball!

The Miracle League of North Orange County is based out of Fullerton, CA and is geared to those who want to play the great game of baseball. We are a nationwide non-profit organization that gives intellectually and physically disabled children and adults the opportunity to play the great game of baseball. Our players will learn the basic fundamentals of baseball on a traditional baseball diamond. We welcome players who are 8 years old and up. There is no age limit and no player will be turned away because of lack of experience, disability or ability to pay. We use soft-core baseballs and will provide Buddy Assistance if needed.
